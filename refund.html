Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which payments made on MentorVerse (courses, internship, webinars, certification programs) may be refunded. By making a purchase, you agree to the policies below.

1. General Policy

All purchases made on MentorVerse are final and follow a strict no-refund policy, except in cases mentioned below.

2. Course Purchases

Refunds are not applicable once:

  • Course content has been viewed or accessed
  • Course PDFs, notes, or videos have been downloaded
  • Login credentials have been used

A refund may be considered only if:

  • The payment was made twice by mistake (duplicate transaction)
  • The amount was deducted but access was not provided
3. Internship Fee Refund

Internship registration fees are non-refundable once the student is onboarded or receives internship material.

Refund is possible only if:

  • Payment was deducted but internship access was not given
  • Duplicate payment occurred
4. Webinar Payments

Paid webinar fees are non-refundable once the registration is confirmed or meeting link is shared.

5. Free Webinars & Free Programs

No payment is taken, so refunds do not apply.

6. Subscription or Membership Plans

Refunds are not applicable for mentorship or membership plans once activated.

7. Technical Issues

If a technical issue prevents you from accessing the product, we will assist you. Refunds may be issued only if:

  • The issue is from MentorVerse’s side
  • We fail to provide access within 7 working days
8. Processing Refunds

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 7–14 working days
  • Amount will be returned to the original payment method only
9. How to Request a Refund

10. Changes to this Policy

MentorVerse reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time without notice.
